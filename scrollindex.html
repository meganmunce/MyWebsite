About Me

I'm currently a junior at Northwestern University studying journalism and political science with an interest in local politics, data visualization and interactive journalism.

Where I Am

As a Silicon Valley native, I've grown up learning the ways technology can make information more accessible. It's my passion to do the same with journalism.

I'm currently a junior at Northwestern University. In June 2022, I'll graduate from the Medill School of Journalism, Media, Integrated Marketing Communications with both my bachelor's in journalism and political science and my master's in journalism with a concentration in Media, Innovation and Content Strategy.

Using the tools of both print and digital journalism, I hope to create stories that are engaging, informative and accesible.
